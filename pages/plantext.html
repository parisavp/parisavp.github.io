Plantext

Overview

An app that helps users to find plants that they grew, read about them and ask their questions.

  • MY ROLE

    UX designer

  • THE TEAM

    2 UX designer
    2 Graphic designer
    4 Engineers

  • DURATION

    3 Month

What was the requirements?

  • A simple way to search plants
  • Including a library of all house plants
  • Using AI to find plants by their picture
  • A forum for users to communicate and ask their questions
  • Mesure the light intensity
  • Search box for finding various plant disease

Persona

Wireframes

Usability Testing

#1

At first, search bar was in the side menu that we thought users might found it complicated to make access. in the other hands, the most important goal of the app is searching plants, so we decided to place the search bar in the first page of the application.

#2

Asking a new question in the forum was also an important capability of the application. So using an icon for accessing, might challenge users for finding it simply. Also a search bar would help users to find questions that might been asked before.

Usability Testing

68% of users lingered on this page for a few moments to understand what is going on. the help button in the top left corner was to small and hard to see at first sight. So we decided to put a help note under the light measure. after that, users had no difficulty to find out how to use the light measure.
